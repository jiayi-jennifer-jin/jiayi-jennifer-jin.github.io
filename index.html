Jiayi Jin

Lecturer in Architecture
Northumbria University

Jiayi Jin is a Lecturer in Architecture at Northumbria University. Her research focuses on gender-inclusive cities, participatory urbanism, and spatial justice, with broader interests in public space, community engagement, and socially responsive urban design.

Her work explores how planning, design, and participatory methods can support more inclusive, equitable, and liveable urban environments.

Research Interests

  • Gender-inclusive cities
  • Participatory urbanism
  • Spatial justice
  • Public space and everyday urban life
  • Community-based design and engagement
